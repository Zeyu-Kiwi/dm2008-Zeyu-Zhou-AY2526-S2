## How did you get it done.
Lucky me that boids was such a famous and popular thing that I was able to find tutorial to create  boids with p5js, so I just follow the tutorial and replicated my own boids. The process made used of many things I learnt from the lesson. For example, class, for loops, array, vector, and etc.

## What can you control
1. Number of boids
2. Adjust cohesion,alignment, and seperation strength
3. Steering force
4. Speed
5. Detection radius (how far the boids can see each others)
6. Triangle boids appearance: height, width, size
7. Toggle bewtween teleport (appear on the other side of screen) or avoid (invisible border around the screen that boids will turn away)
8. Color mode: single (white) or heading (change color according to direction)
9. Toggle visibility of QuadTree (optimization that allow more boids but still maintain the performance)
10. Reset button
